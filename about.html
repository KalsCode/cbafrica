History

Computers and Beyond Africa started in 2010 emerging from an IT business enterprise providing services in East and Central Africa known as Tech Support Services. We set out to develop and implement IT enterprise solutions for small, medium and large scale enterprises. We have come a long way continually establishing reputation and crafting strong lasting business relationships on the principle of trust through investing in people and ethical business practices.

Strategy

Vision

To become the leading IT and Integrated solutions provider in Uganda and the East African Community

Mission Statement

Provide high quality, easily accessible and timely solutions for the technology.

Business Strategy

We work with our customers in a partnership style of approach to provide the IT assistance required. Together we will work to drive down costs on IT infrastructure and integrate all your IT systems to ensure near to zero operational down time.
